Our Story

Abujhad contractors was established in 2009 as a construction company by its CEO, Mr. Lule Ismail Bulangiti. Since its inception, the company has been offering construction services both domestically and internationally. Abujhadi contractors has successfully completed several projects, including Makindye Market, Busega Market, BMK House, and Africana Apartments, among others.

In 2014, recognizing the rising demand for raw materials at their construction sites, the CEO proposed the idea of launching a hardware distribution business. This venture proved to be successful, and Abujhadi now operates as both a construction company and a distributor of hardware materials, serving both domestic and international markets.

Currently, the company is actively supplying hardware materials for various projects, including Nakivubo Stadium, Maya Nature Resort, an Agro processing plant in Entebbe, and numerous other ongoing projects.

About the CEO Abujhad contractors Limited

Mr. Lule Ismail Bulangiti is a distinguished civil engineer with a remarkable track record in the construction industry. As the CEO of Abujhad contractors Limited, he has demonstrated exceptional leadership and expertise in overseeing construction projects of varying scales. With a career spanning several decades, Mr. Bulangiti has earned a reputation for delivering high-quality, innovative, and sustainable construction solutions.

OTHER RESPONSBILITIES

In addition to his role as CEO, Mr. Bulangiti also serves as the Public Relations Officer (PRO) of the Construction Hardware and Dealer Association (CHADA). In this capacity, he plays a pivotal role in bridging communication between construction hardware suppliers and industry professionals. His dedication to fostering strong relationships and promoting industry excellence through CHADA is highly commendable.

Remark

Mr. Bulangiti's commitment to advancing the construction sector through innovation, professionalism, and collaboration has made him a respected figure in the industry. His multifaceted role as a civil engineer, CEO, and PRO showcases his versatility and dedication to the betterment of the construction community. With his passion for excellence and a strong commitment to quality, he continues to be a driving force behind Abujhd contractors Limited and CHADA, leaving a lasting impact on the construction and hardware industry.
